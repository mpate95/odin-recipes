Corn On The Cob

Corn on the cob is cooked in boiled water with a little sugar and lemon juice for the sweetest, tastiest corn! My mother-in-law gave me this easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Lime
  • Chile

Steps

  1. Shuck the corn
  2. Boil some water with salt
  3. Place corn in the pot and turn off the heat
  4. Garnish with butter, salt, lime, and chile
